About Powell

Built on Faith,
Driven by Purpose

Powell Parker is the founder and managing member of Rightful Returns Recovery — a contingency-only surplus funds and forensic asset recovery firm operating across Texas, Arizona, and Georgia. Every case is taken at zero upfront cost. Every recovery is built on integrity.


Rooted in the small-town values of Hereford, Texas and the wide-open spirit of the Arizona desert, Powell brings a cowboy work ethic and a servant's heart to every client relationship. Faith, family, and community aren't just words — they're the foundation everything is built on.

Arizona Roots

State 48.
Home Forever.

Raised in Queen Creek, Arizona — before it was a suburb, when it was still red dirt and cotton fields. Powell grew up competing in High School and College Rodeos across the state, specializing in Team Roping and Bulldogging.


Arizona didn't just shape Powell's work ethic — it forged his identity. The desert teaches patience. The rodeo arena teaches resilience. And the community of Queen Creek taught him that your word is your bond and your handshake still means something.
