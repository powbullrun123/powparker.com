About Powell

Built on Faith,
Driven by Purpose

Powell Parker is the founder and managing member of Rightful Returns Recovery — a contingency-only surplus funds and forensic asset recovery firm operating across Texas, Arizona, and Georgia. Every case is taken at zero upfront cost. Every recovery is built on integrity.


Rooted in the small-town values of Hereford, Texas and the wide-open spirit of the Arizona desert, Powell brings a cowboy work ethic and a servant's heart to every client relationship. Faith, family, and community aren't just words — they're the foundation everything is built on.

Arizona Roots

State 48.
Home Forever.

Raised in Queen Creek, Arizona — before it was a suburb, when it was still red dirt and cotton fields. The kind of place where you knew every neighbor and your word was your bond.


Powell competed in High School and College Rodeos across the state, specializing in Team Roping and Bulldogging. The arena didn't just build skill — it built character. Preparation wins. Effort never lies. You always get back up.


The desert teaches patience. The rodeo arena teaches resilience. And Queen Creek taught him that a handshake still means something.
