About Powell

Built on Faith,
Driven by Purpose

Powell Parker is the founder and managing member of Rightful Returns Recovery — a contingency-only surplus funds and forensic asset recovery firm operating across Texas, Arizona, and Georgia. Every case is taken at zero upfront cost. Every recovery is built on integrity.


Rooted in the small-town values of Hereford, Texas and the wide-open spirit of the Arizona desert, Powell brings a cowboy work ethic and a servant's heart to every client relationship. Faith, family, and community aren't just words — they're the foundation everything is built on.

Arizona Roots

State 48.
Home Forever.

Raised in Queen Creek, Arizona — before it was a suburb, when it was still red dirt and cotton fields. The kind of place where you knew your neighbors and your word still meant something.


Powell competed in High School and College Rodeos across the state, specializing in Team Roping and Bulldogging. The arena didn't just build skill — it built character. It taught him that preparation wins, effort never lies, and you always get back up.


Arizona didn't just shape Powell's work ethic — it forged his identity. The desert teaches patience. The rodeo arena teaches resilience. Queen Creek taught him that your handshake still means something.
